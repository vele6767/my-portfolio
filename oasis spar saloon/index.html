Visit Us & FAQs

Find us conveniently located within the Marriott building in Warsaw. We are open Monday to Friday, and here are answers to some common questions.

Where are you located?
+
We are located at Al. Jerozolimskie 65/79, Level -1, Shop 20, LIM Centre Passage inside the Marriott building in Warsaw (00-697). Take the escalator down to the -1 shopping level.
What are your opening hours?
+
Monday–Friday: 08:30–20:30 · Saturday: 09:00–17:00 · Sunday: 10:00–21:00. Walk-ins welcome, appointments recommended for peak times and VIP packages.
How do I book an appointment?
+
Call us at +48 883 529 791 or +48 22 630 6470. You can also email us at kontakt@oasisbeautydayspa.pl or message us on Facebook or Instagram @oasisbeauty_dayspa.
Do you speak English?
+
Yes! Our team is fully bilingual in Polish and English. All international guests are warmly welcomed.
What is the VIP Sauna Suite?
+
Our private sauna suite is exclusively yours for the session — no shared spaces, complete privacy. Perfect for couples or solo relaxation. Ask us about availability when booking.
Do you sell gift vouchers?
+
Yes! Gift vouchers are available in any amount. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or corporate gifts. Contact us to arrange your voucher.

Ready for your urban escape? Book an appointment or reach out to us directly. We are located in the Marriott building, available Mon–Fri 08:30–20:30.
